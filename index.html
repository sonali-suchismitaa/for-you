Steps

  1. Boil the Maggi noodles.
  2. Meanwhile, finely chop some garlic.
  3. Once the noodles are boiled, strain them and set aside.
  4. In a kadhai, add butter, the chopped garlic, Schezwan sauce, ketchup, salt, black pepper powder, oregano, chilli flakes, magic masala, and Maggi masala.
  5. Mix everything well until the spices and sauces combine smoothly.
  6. Add a little water to create a gravy-like texture.
  7. Add the strained Maggi noodles to the kadhai and mix well with the sauce.
  8. Plate the Maggi nicely.
  9. Now, to prepare the sunny side up egg:
  10. Add some butter to the kadhai (this helps give the egg a circular shape).
  11. Crack the egg into the kadhai, keeping the yolk intact.
  12. Let the egg cook uncovered for 2 minutes, then cover it and cook for another 2 minutes.
  13. Carefully place the cooked sunny side up egg on top of the Maggi.
  14. Sprinkle some salt, black pepper powder, oregano, and chilli flakes over the egg.
